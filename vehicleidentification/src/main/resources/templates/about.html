ABOUT

Vehicle Identification is a website that offers the functionality to search about the owner and vehicle details by registration number.

It also offers additional functionality to the admin such as adding, updating and deleting existing information.

Moreover, the vehicle details could also be searched by uploading an image of the vehicle's license plate.
